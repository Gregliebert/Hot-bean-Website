Employee Roles

Learn more about what our team members do every day at The Hot Beans.

Rachel - Chief Executive Officer

Department: Management

As the CEO, Rachel is responsible for the overall success of the company. She doesn't just sit in an office; she is involved in planning the future of The Hot Beans.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Meeting with big clients to sign contracts.
  • Making financial decisions and setting budgets.
  • Hiring new staff members.
  • Ensuring all projects are finished on time.

James - Senior Web Developer

Department: IT / Engineering

James is the technical brain behind our websites. He writes the code that makes websites actually work, dealing with databases and servers.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Writing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code.
  • Fixing bugs and errors in the software.
  • Managing the company servers.
  • Updating websites for existing clients.

Thomas - Lead Designer

Department: Creative

Thomas makes sure everything looks good. He decides which colors, fonts, and images to use so that users have a good experience.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Creating mockups and wireframes for new sites.
  • Designing logos and banners.
  • Selecting color schemes for clients.
  • Ensuring websites are easy to use on mobile phones.
